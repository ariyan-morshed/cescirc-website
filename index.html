Our Vision

The CESC International Relations Club serves as the premier academic arena for developing diplomatic acumen, geopolitical awareness, and rigorous research capabilities. We transcend standard curriculum limits, molding students into analytical global citizens prepared for the complexities of international affairs.

The CESCIRC Roadmap (2026)

Phase I: Foundational

  • 1st Session: What is International Relations and Why It Matters
  • 2nd Session: Foundations of International Relations
  • 3rd Session: Understanding World Politics

Phase II: Technical

  • 4th Session: Current Geopolitical Issues in South Asia
  • 5th Session: Diplomacy & Negotiation
  • 6th Session: Introduction to Model United Nations

Phase III: Application

  • 7th Session: Resolution Drafting & MUN Workshop
  • 8th Session: Mock Model United Nations

About CESCIRC

Starting its journey in 2026, the CESC International Relations Club was entirely conceptualized and built by the students of Cantonment English School and College. It serves as the premier academic arena for developing diplomatic acumen and geopolitical awareness.
