About CESCIRC

Starting its journey in 2026, the CESC International Relations Club was entirely conceptualized and built by the students of Cantonment English School and College. It serves as the premier academic arena for developing diplomatic acumen, geopolitical awareness, and rigorous research capabilities through MUN conferences and structured discourse.
