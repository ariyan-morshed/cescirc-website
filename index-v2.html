INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS CLUB

Your gateway to global affairs.

OUR VISION

To develop students' awareness of historical and contemporary geopolitical developments around the world. We offer a structured academic environment where members build research, writing, debate, and negotiation skills, thereby shaping them into knowledgeable, diplomatic, and globally conscious citizens.

CLUB SESSIONS

Term 1: Foundational Sessions

  • What is International Relations and Why It Matters
  • Foundations of International Relations
  • Understanding World Politics

Term 2: Technical Sessions

  • Current Geopolitical Issues in South Asia
  • Diplomacy & Negotiation
  • Introduction to Model United Nations

Term 3: Application Sessions

  • Resolution Drafting & MUN Workshop
  • Mock Model United Nations
